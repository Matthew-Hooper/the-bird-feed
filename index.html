Welcome to the Bird Feed

In the navbar you will find two links. The first will take you to a page for random facts about birds.

The second link will give you a set of three bird pictures.

I came up with the idea for this site mainly by searching for animal APIs to mess around with. I'm very fond of birds and when I found an API for facts and images, I knew I had to do it. The nerd in me was excited to make use of the APIs, especially for random facts no one cares about, and the bird lover in me jumped on in.
